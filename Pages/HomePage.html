About us

is a graduation project for a group of students at Princess Nourah University. It is a fake news detection system using

machine learning (ML) algorithms. Yaqn aims to contribute to limit the spread of fake news by providing two essential services: a news detection service, and report fake news. Both services are English and Arabic. Yaqn is an arabic word that means "someone who makes sure something is true". The word was chosen as it expresses the purpose of the system.
